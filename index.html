World's Strongest Man

The World's Strongest Man is an international Strongman competition. Organized by American event management company IMG, a subsidiary of Endeavor, it is broadcast in the US during summers.

Arnold strongman classic

The Arnold Strongman Classic is an annual competition featuring strength athletes from all over the world, determining who is the Strongest Man in the World. Created by Arnold Schwarzenegger, Jim Lorimer and Terry Todd.

Rogue invitational

The Rogue Invitational is an annual sporting event that includes a CrossFit competition and a strongman competition. The event, organized by Rogue, started in 2019 as a CrossFit sanctioned competition.
